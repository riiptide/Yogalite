Privacy Policy

Yogalite

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Overview

Yogalite is designed to be a private, lightweight yoga companion. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Information Stored on Your Device

Yogalite stores some information locally on your device so the app can remember your preferences and progress:

  • Your display name, practice time preference, experience level, and selected goals or focus areas.
  • Your saved practices, favorite flows, completed practices, streaks, and minutes practiced.
  • An optional profile photo if you choose one from your photo library.
  • Anonymous product interaction events, such as daily activity, completed practices, favorite flow changes, retention milestones, and possible crash indicators.

Photos

If you choose a profile photo, Yogalite uses Apple's photo picker so you can select an image. The selected and cropped photo is stored locally in the app. Yogalite does not upload your photo to a server.

Analytics

Yogalite records simple anonymous product analytics on your device to help understand app usage, such as completed practices and favorite flow changes. These analytics are not linked to your identity and are not used for advertising or tracking.

Sharing and Third Parties

Yogalite does not sell your personal information. Yogalite does not share your information with advertisers or third-party tracking services.

Deleting Your Data

You can remove locally stored Yogalite data by deleting the app from your device. If you reinstall Yogalite, locally stored preferences and practice history may no longer be available.

Children's Privacy

Yogalite is a general wellness app and is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as Yogalite changes. If the app begins collecting or sharing additional types of data, this policy will be updated to explain those changes.

Contact

For privacy questions about Yogalite, contact the developer using the support contact listed on Yogalite's App Store page or on the website where this policy is hosted.
